About André Hazes Jr.

André Hazes Jr. is a Dutch singer and television presenter. He is the son of the late André Hazes, a well-known Dutch singer. Hazes Jr. has followed in his father's footsteps and has become a popular figure in the Dutch music scene.

Music

  • Wijzer (2017)
  • Anders (2018)
  • Kwijt (2019)
